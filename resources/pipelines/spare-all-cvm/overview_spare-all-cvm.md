### SPARE-CVM Scores

Comprehensive pipeline to generate all Cardiovascular and Metabolic related SPARE scores that could be calculated from T1w + FLAIR head MRI images.

##### List of SPARE scores:

- Type 2 Diabetes (T2D)
- Hyperlipidemia (HL)
- Obestiy (OB)
- Smoking Status (SM)

#### Input

- T1-weighted & FLAIR head MRI scan pairs (Nifti)
- Entry or a CSV file containing:
	- Demographics (Age, Sex)
	- Cognitive Status (IsCN)


#### Output

- All available SPARE-CVM (Cardiovascular and metabolic disease) scores (CSV)
